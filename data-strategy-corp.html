Data Strategy Brings Tangible Benefits

Impaired data strategy means impaired management and reduced ability to compete in the high volatility environment of today.

  • Traditional data sharing channels are disjointed and cause delays and poor analysis (Excel, PowerPoint, Email). Realtime information flows direct from the company to the stakeholders drives much better decision making.
  • According to research, adoption of a cohesive data strategy has driven a tangible on corporate performance both in terms of share price (and thus ROE) as well as on financial margins.
  • Source: The Impact of Big Data on Firm Performance, Univ. of Quebec, Ertz et al, Mar-2021

Key Drivers are Business Growth

Data Strategy is not a cost - it's an investment.

  • Mid market firms have typically been behind the curve when it comes to adopting data strategy and using business intelligence tools
  • The reasons for this are varied but usually come down to cost and tech led projects instead of business led projects
  • Where implementation is done, it is usually not customized adequately (again due to cost) and so the exercise ends up ineffective PowerPoint, Email). Realtime information flows direct from the company to the stakeholders drives much better decision making.

Further on to Digital Transformation

Data strategy helps improve information flows and makes real time data available - and more importantly - makes insights and human readable information available to senior management, shareholders, investors and lenders.

  • The implementation of BI becomes the first step in preparing a data strategy for the firm.
  • BI tools are a low risk/ low cost/ low disruption entry point into the Industry 4.0 journey.
